General Information

Tomatis® Listening Therapy
(Auditory Simulation)

Scientists and Practitioners now agree the brain can undergo significant and permanent changes. This is commonly referred to as neuroplasticity Using this principle DR Alfred Tomatis developed what is now called the Tomatis® listening therapy

• This therapy delivers music, via wireless headphones, through the ear and through bone conduction. The music is filtered and gated.

• It produces sounds that change unexpectedly.

• This music “wakes up” the brain and encourages neuron growth.

• This music also exercises the muscles of the ear which transmit the signals to the brain.

• The pathways become stronger and help cause a permanent change.

Who would benefit from a course of Tomatis® Listening Therapy?

Those who present with symptoms of ADD, ADHD, FAS or APD have all been shown to benefit greatly.
Those who have trouble concentrating.
Those who worry too much and/or have issues with anxiety.
Those with coordination issues can make considerable improvement.
Those of all ages and stages can benefit.

Please see the attached research and I would recommend a quick search on the internet, I have also attached some website addresses to help you, to verify these claims.

What does Tomatis® Look Like?
Participants would wear wireless headphones, with the music coming through them, for ideally 2 hours a day for 13 days consecutively.
There are 2 further intensives to follow one usually at about 4 weeks after the first and the third about 8 weeks following the second.
Times and dates can be flexible depending on the child and the individual results.
Participants can hear while they are wearing the earphones and can participate in class if that is what is required.
